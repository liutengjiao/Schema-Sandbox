# Contributing

Schema Sandbox is currently maintained as a public methodology and draft specification project.

## Useful contribution types

- Clarifying terminology
- Improving attribution language
- Proposing SIP-Core fields
- Adding industrial middleware reference patterns
- Improving schema examples
- Reporting unsafe or ambiguous examples

## Contribution principles

1. Keep the public draft implementation-neutral.
2. Avoid overclaiming production readiness.
3. Separate methodology from runtime implementation.
4. Prefer clear examples over broad claims.
5. Do not submit proprietary customer workflows, confidential security rules, or private prompt chains.

## Licensing of contributions

By contributing, you agree that your documentation contributions may be distributed under CC BY 4.0 and your code/schema contributions may be distributed under Apache-2.0 unless otherwise stated.
