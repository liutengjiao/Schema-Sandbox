## What attribution does not imply

Attribution does not imply:

- Endorsement by the original authors
- Certification by Schema Sandbox
- Official compatibility
- Security approval
- Permission to use Schema Sandbox or SIP as your product name

## Trademark note

You may describe your work as "based on" or "inspired by" Schema Sandbox. You may not use Schema Sandbox, SchemaSandbox, SIP, or confusingly similar marks as product names or certification marks without separate permission.
