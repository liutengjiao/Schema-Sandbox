Publication of SIP-Core materials does not create any obligation to provide:

- hosted service access;

- marketplace listing;
- certification, verification, or endorsement.

## Commercial Sandbox Packs

Third-party or first-party Sandbox Packs distributed through psi.run may be
subject to separate commercial licenses, revenue-share terms, publisher review,
security review, or marketplace policies.

## Conformance is not certification

A project may accurately state that it implements a public SIP-Core version if it
does so truthfully. However, "certified", "verified", "official", or
"psi.run-approved" claims require separate written permission.
